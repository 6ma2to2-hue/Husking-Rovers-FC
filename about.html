About Husking Rovers

Husking Rovers FC was founded in 2022 in the heart of Badalona, Spain. Built from a vision of unity and sporting excellence, the club quickly rose to prominence as a beacon of ambition for the community.

The philosophy of Husking Rovers is grounded in perseverance, fair play, and the belief that effort and teamwork pave the way for victory. Players are developed through a modern academy system that focuses not only on skill but also on character and leadership.

Since its foundation, the club has embraced a strong identity as "The Lions of Badalona," symbolizing courage, strength, and the roar of a community standing together.

Beyond the pitch, Husking Rovers have engaged in social programs that support youth, education, and local culture, turning the club into a pillar of the city’s identity. Every match at Husking Stadium is more than a sporting event—it is a gathering of families, traditions, and pride.
