Location

Husking Rovers are based in Badalona, a city on the northeastern coast of Spain, bordering Barcelona. The location offers a perfect mix of Mediterranean charm, cultural heritage, and easy access to Catalonia’s football traditions.

Fans from across the region travel to Husking Stadium, making matchday a celebration that unites diverse communities under one banner.
